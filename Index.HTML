￼ Order via Official AHA Materials Portal

                

Entry will be flatly denied without your physical/digital workbook on class day. No walk-in spares are provided.

            
        
        
                                  
        
                         
                
Payment & Cancellation Framework
                

All transactions are completely non-refundable. Registrants may request a rescheduling modification exactly one (1) time within 15 days of transaction date, provided the new date falls within 30 days of that initial rescheduling request window.

                                 
Grace Period & Attendance Rules
                

A strict 10-minute maximum late grace period is applied. Once past this window, structural safety doors lock automatically, and late entries are classified as forfeited no-shows.

                                 
Rescheduling Limitations
                

Rescheduling notices must be formally submitted a full 24 hours prior to the class start times. Late notices trigger an automatic no-show status charge.

                                 
Class Behavior & Quiet Etiquette
                

All cellular phones and communications tech must be put onto silent modes during structural teaching hours. Students must interact with total integrity, mutual respect, and high focus. Disruptions forfeit your seat without refunds.

                                 
Testing Metrics & Ratios
                

Instruction follows a max 8:1 student-to-instructor ratio. Regular tactical skill check-offs occur during progression ahead of your required final text validations. Digital certificates dispatch within 24–48 hours of successful completion.
